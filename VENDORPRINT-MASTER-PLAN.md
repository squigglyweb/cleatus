# PART 2: THE VENDORPRINT SOLUTION

## The System We Build

### 1. Client Portal (The Front End)

**For the Admin/Executive:**
- Dashboard showing all employees, their card status, last order date
- One-click reorder for individual or bulk
- Analytics: cards ordered this month, cost per employee, trends
- Multiple templates by department/role (auto-select based on title)
- Terminated employee card management (mark as inactive)
- Upload CSV for bulk orders

**For the Employee (Self-Service):**
- Simple form: name, title, department, phone, email
- Preview their card before print
- Request button (goes to admin for approval)
- Order status tracking

### 2. The Proofing System (Backend)

- Customer uploads logo/design OR uses our templates
- System generates digital proof within 24 hours
- Customer approves online (click-to-approve)
- Proof stored for reorders (auto-match to template)
- Revision tracking (did they ask for changes?)

### 3. Print Partners

- We broker to 2-3 quality commercial printers
- Get bulk pricing (500 cards = ~$12-18/unit)
- Pass through savings to client
- Quality control before shipment

### 4. Delivery

- Standard shipping (5-7 days)
- Rush (2-3 days)
- Local pickup or delivery

- $49/month for agencies to use our proofing portal

- **Risk:** They might find cheaper printers

### Option B: Print-Commitment Model (RECOMMENDED)
- Free access to proofing system
- Must print through us (minimum 50 jobs/month)
- Guarantees print volume
- **This is the winner** - tie the tool to the print

### Option C: Per-Proof Fee
- $5-10 per proof generated
- Small barrier to entry
- Could work for high-volume agencies

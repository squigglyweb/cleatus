### The Story You Don't Hear

The name "Waxhaw" comes from the Waxhaw people — an indigenous tribe that lived in this area long before anyone put us on a map. They were related to the Catawba, and they had a practice that's... honestly unforgettable.

They flattened the foreheads of their infants.

Starting at birth, they'd bind the baby's head to a flat board. The wider eyes that resulted were said to give them a hunting advantage. It's the kind of fact that makes you realize — this land has seen some things.

The tribe is gone now, absorbed or displaced like so many others. But the name stuck. And honestly? That's part of what makes this place historic. We've got roots that run deeper than the shopping centers on Providence Road would have you believe.

### Why We're Here

Here's the thing: we believe local business is the heartbeat of a community. Not the big boxes, not the national chains — the owner who remembers your name, the restaurant that's been serving the same recipe for 30 years, the mechanic who actually explains what's wrong with your car.

The Local NearBuy exists to champion those businesses. And here's the twist that makes us different:

**Every listing, every ad, every offer on NearBuy helps feed families — both right here in Waxhaw and around the world.**

We call it Glocal: local impact + global reach. When you support a local business through NearBuy, you're not just keeping money in the community — you're helping provide meals to kids and families who need it. We've already provided thousands of meals through our partner organizations, and it's all because neighbors like you choose local first.

That's a pretty good feeling, right?

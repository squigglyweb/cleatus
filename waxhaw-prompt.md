# Waxhaw T-Shirt Design Prompt

## Whimsical Style

**Prompt:**
```
A black and white ink sketch illustration of Waxhaw, North Carolina from an overhead bird's eye view. Railroad tracks split the town right down the middle, running horizontally with a historic wooden railroad bridge crossing over the tracks at the center. Charming storefronts and shops line both sides of the tracks creating a main street feel. An iconic water tower overlooks the town. Includes a small town hall, church with steeple, park with gazebo and playground, houses, trees, and curved railroad tracks winding through. Quaint, nostalgic, folk art style, clean linework, detailed but not cluttered, t-shirt design ready, white background
```

**Negative prompt:**
```
color, colorful, photo, realistic, modern, gradient, shadow, messy, cluttered, text, words, logos
```

---

## For reference - key elements to include:
- Railroad tracks splitting town middle
- Storefronts on both sides (main street)
- Historic wooden bridge overhead
- Water tower
- Town hall, church, park with gazebo
- Black and white ink sketch style

---

## Tips for use:
- Use in Midjourney with: --style raw or --stylize 250
- For DALL-E: can be shorter, focus on key elements

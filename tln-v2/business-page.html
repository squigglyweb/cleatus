About 🤖 AI Written

Mama's Pizzeria has been a Waxhaw staple since 1998, serving authentic New York-style pizza made with recipes passed down through three generations. Our dough is hand-tossed daily, our sauce is made from scratch using San Marzano tomatoes, and we source our mozzarella from a local dairy. Whether you're craving a classic pepperoni, a loaded meat lovers, or something creative, our pizzas are crafted with love and the finest ingredients. Bring the family, grab a seat by our fireplace, and taste the difference that 25+ years of dedication makes.

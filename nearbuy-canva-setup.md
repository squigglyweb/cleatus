# Canva Setup for The Local NearBuy

## Your Brand Colors
- **Primary:** #d32f2f (red)
- **Secondary:** #1a1a1a (charcoal)
- **Background:** #faf8f5 (cream)
- **Accent:** #c9a66b (gold/coffee)

## How to Set Up in Canva

### 1. Create a Brand Kit
- Go to Canva → Brand → Brand Kit
- Add these colors
- Upload your logo (once you have final files)

### 2. Create These Template Categories

---

## Template 1: Business Spotlight (MAIN)

**Dimensions:** 1080 x 1080 (square)

**Canva Prompt:**
```
Create an Instagram post template for a local business spotlight. 
Style: Modern, clean, warm local feel.
Background: Light cream (#faf8f5)
Accent color: Deep red (#d32f2f)
Layout: Photo placeholder at top (70% height), business info card at bottom.
Include: "NearBuy" logo text top left, category pill top right.
Text area for business name, location, and a short quote/review.
Placeholders clearly marked for: PHOTO, BUSINESS NAME, ADDRESS, QUOTE, CATEGORY
```

## Quick Tips

1. **Make one "Master" template** — Duplicate and swap colors for each category
2. **Use text placeholders** — In Canva, click on text → Accessibility → Alt Text to note what's there
3. **Save to "Brand Templates"** — Makes it easy to find later
4. **Create a folder** — "NearBuy Content" to keep all templates organized

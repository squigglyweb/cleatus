What is The Local NearBuy?

We're a hyperlocal media brand built for Greater Waxhaw and surrounding communities. Our mission: help you discover the best local businesses while making a real difference.

Every recommendation we make, every business we feature, every ad we run — it all comes back to supporting our community. That's the NearBuy difference.

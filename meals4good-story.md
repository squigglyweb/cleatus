# My Story - The Beginning

I spent 30+ years designing. But I wanted to build something that actually *mattered*.

I created The Local NearBuy — but it was missing something. So I walked away.

Then one night at dinner, a taco place said "Buy a meal, we'll give a meal to a hungry kid."

Lightbulb.

**Help local businesses. Help kids. That's it.**

- 1 person dies every 4 seconds from hunger
- 828 million go to bed hungry every night

**Now it's your turn.**

**Provide meals. Help kids. Donate today.**

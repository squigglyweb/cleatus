# My Story - The Beginning

30+ years as a graphic designer. But I wanted to build something that actually mattered.

I created The Local NearBuy — a way to help local businesses shine. But something was missing. I couldn't connect the "doing good" part.

So I put it down. Walked away.

Then one night, my family went to dinner. The taco place said: "Buy a meal, we'll give a meal to a hungry kid."

"Ok, cool," I thought.

But later that night — rethinking my day — it hit me. My old idea wasn't dead. It just needed a purpose.

**Help businesses.** (They're awesome and deserve to be seen)
**Help kids.** (No child should go hungry)

That's it. That's the whole thing.

---

## The Facts

- 1 person dies every 4 seconds from hunger
- 828 million people go to bed hungry every night
- Kids in our own communities are affected

## The Goal

- Connect local businesses with people who want to help
- Every purchase through NearBuy = meals for kids
- Build Meals4Good into something bigger than both of us

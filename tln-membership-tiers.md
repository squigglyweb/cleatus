# The Local NearBuy — Membership Tiers

## Pro Membership

### Postcard Exposure
- **Small ad spot in every campaign** — Pro members get a small spot (3.8"x2.5") in every postcard campaign we run. If more businesses want in than spots available, rotation applies. Consistent exposure every month without asking.

### Directory Profile — Custom Features
- **Custom brand colors** — Match their brand (primary color, accent color) on their profile page
- **Custom hero header** — Their own header image/banner at the top of their profile
- **Evergreen offer** — One offer that's always live (can update anytime)
- **One additional offer** — A second offer they can rotate or seasonal
- **5-image gallery** — Show off their space, products, team
- **Social media channels** — Link to their Facebook, Instagram, etc.
- **One video** — Embed a YouTube/video intro or tour

### Lead Access
- **Basic dashboard** — Shows how many people claimed their offer
- **Lead info** — Name and email of people who claimed (these are opted-in leads from the QR scan)
- **Scan count** — Just the raw number of times their QR was scanned

### Directory Presence
- **"Pro Member" badge** — Credibility marker on their listing (looks professional, not "solicitor")

### Events
- **One event listing per month** — Add to the directory calendar (grand opening, special night, class, etc.)

### Analytics
- **Basic QR analytics** — Scan count + claim date. Shows the campaign is working without needing a full dashboard.

### Newsletter
- **"Member Spotlight"** — 1-line mention in the newsletter when they join. Low cost to us, feels like a real welcome to the community.

## Pro+ Membership

Everything in Pro, PLUS:

- **Larger/better postcard spots** — Priority over Pro for spot size and placement
- **Unlimited offers** — No cap on rotating offers
- **Full analytics dashboard** — Detailed breakdown of scans, claims, redemptions, time-of-day, device types
- **Priority placement** — Featured at top of directory category
- **More gallery images** — Unlimited gallery vs 5
- **More videos** — Multiple video embeds
- **Dedicated landing page** — A standalone page (not just a profile), great for seasonal promotions or new products
- **Priority support** — Faster response time

---

## Key Differentiator

**Pro** = Complete, valuable, "we've got you covered."  
**Pro+** = For businesses that want maximum exposure and data.

The gap makes the sale easy: "Pro gives you everything you need. Pro+ pushes you to the front of the line and gives you the data to prove it's working."

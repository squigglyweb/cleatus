## VISUAL NOTES

- Accent: Warm orange (#ed8936) - action buttons
- Background: Off-white (#f7fafc)
- Text: Dark grey (#2d3748)

**Photography Style:**

- Show people working, not shaking hands in meetings
- Warm, approachable - not corporate

**Typography:**
- Clean sans-serif (Inter or Poppins)
- Large headlines, easy to scan
- Short paragraphs

---

## HOW TO NOT CONFUSE PEOPLE

**The Golden Rule:** ONE headline, ONE action per section.

**When people get confused:**
- It's because they don't know what to do next
- So every section has ONE clear button

**The flow:**
1. Home says: "See if we're a good fit" → Quiz
2. Quiz says: "Schedule a call" → Calendar
3. Services says: "See pricing" → Pricing page
4. Pricing says: "Ready? Let's go" → Contact

**No dead ends.** Every page leads somewhere.

## Summer Camps & Day Programs — Greater Waxhaw Area

Summer's coming. Your kids need something worth waking up for.

Whether they're into sports, tech, horses, art, or just burning energy with other kids — here's what's running in the Waxhaw area this summer.

### Sports & Active Camps

**Coach Conroy's All-Sports Camp** — Co-ed, age-grouped activities with sports-specific instruction. Enthusiastic staff, new experiences every day. [Learn more]

**Introductory Soccer Camp (June & July sessions)** — Kids learn the basics through games, scrimmages, and fun drills. Make friends, gain confidence, love the sport. [Learn more]

**Tennis Revolution (NEW)** — Indoor 10U program with kid-friendly equipment, smaller courts, and games that actually teach serving and rallying. Confidence-builder. [Learn more]

**Charlotte Allstar Gymnastics & Cheerleading** — Gymnastics or cheerleading with games, crafts, and themed days (Tattoo Tuesday, Glow-in-the-Dark Thursday, Inflatable Fridays). Half-day and full-day options. $250/week, runs June 1 - August 21. [Learn more]

**Charlotte's Best Afterschool & Summer Camps** — Parkour, martial arts, fitness, and high-energy games. Kids build confidence, coordination, and leadership in a safe environment. [Learn more]

### STEM & Tech Camps

**CAMMP (Think, Build, Create)** — Full-day blend of math + robotics/coding. Half-day each, hands-on problem-solving, creativity through play. Age-grouped. Runs August at the new Waxhaw Learning Center. [Learn more]

**Club SciKidz** — 70+ themed camps covering robotics, coding, Minecraft, Vet Med, Drones, and more. Ages 4-15. $375-$395/week. Multiple Charlotte locations. [Learn more]

**Camp Tech Revolution** — Tech-focused camps for ages 6-14. Sessions June 15 - July 31. Fusion Academy Toringdon Way (Ballantyne area). [Learn more]

**IDEA Lab Kids Ballantyne** — STEAM-powered camps: cooking, robotics, drones, 3D printing. Ages 4-13. [Learn more]

**Terrific Scientific** — 40+ hands-on science and engineering camps. Grades K-7. Multiple locations. [Learn more]

**Mad Science** — Hands-on STEM experiments and interactive science. PreK through 6th grade. Various locations, June 8 - August 7. [Learn more]

### Arts & Creative Camps

**Children's Art Classes** — Award-winning art school in Fort Mill. Summer workshops for all ages. [Learn more]

**Matthews Playhouse of the Performing Arts** — Theatre camp serving Union County since 1995. Ages 4+. [Learn more]

**Charlotte Ballet Academy** — Storybook Summer Camps bring favorite stories to life through dance. Ages 3-10, no experience needed. Final day performance for families. [Learn more]

**Flour Power Kids Cooking Studios** — Culinary camps for young chefs. Hands-on, fun, educational. Fort Mill location. [Learn more]

**Camp Mindy at the JCC** — Traditional day camp on a 54-acre campus in South Charlotte. Activities, friendships, outdoor time. Open to all. [Learn more]

**Town of Matthews Parks & Recreation** — Full-day and half-day camps. Ages 4-17. Budget-friendly option with variety. [Learn more]

**Charlotte Country Day Summer Programs** — 150+ camps across academics, arts, and sports. Ages 4-18. Starts at $365. [Learn more]

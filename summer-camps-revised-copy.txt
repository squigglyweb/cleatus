## Summer Camps & Day Programs — Greater Waxhaw Area

Summer's coming. Your kids need something worth waking up for.

Whether they're into sports, tech, horses, or just burning energy with other kids — here's what's running in the Waxhaw area this summer.

### Sports & Active Camps

**Coach Conroy's All-Sports Camp** — Co-ed, age-grouped activities with sports-specific instruction. Enthusiastic staff, new experiences every day. [Learn more]

**Introductory Soccer Camp (June & July sessions)** — Kids learn the basics through games, scrimmages, and fun drills. Make friends, gain confidence, love the sport. [Learn more]

**Tennis Revolution (NEW)** — Indoor 10U program with kid-friendly equipment, smaller courts, and games that actually teach serving and rallying. Confidence-builder. [Learn more]

### Specialty Camps

**Thunderbay Riding Academy** — Day camps and advanced sessions. Kids dive deeper into horsemanship, horse care, and saddle time — not just lessons. [Learn more]

**CAMMP (Think, Build, Create)** — Full-day blend of math + robotics/coding. Half-day each, hands-on problem-solving, creativity through play. Age-grouped. Runs August at the new Waxhaw Learning Center. Decades of trusted programs in the Charlotte area. [Learn more]

---

*All camps listed are local to the Greater Waxhaw area — Marvin, Weddington, Wesley Chapel, Indian Land, and Ballantney.*

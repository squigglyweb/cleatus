## Summer Camps & Day Programs — Greater Waxhaw Area

Summer's coming. Your kids need something worth waking up for.

Whether they're into sports, tech, horses, art, or just burning energy with other kids — here's what's running in the Waxhaw area this summer.

### Sports & Active Camps

**Coach Conroy's All-Sports Camp** — Co-ed, age-grouped activities with sports-specific instruction. Enthusiastic staff, new experiences every day. [Register]

**Introductory Soccer Camp (June & July sessions)** — Kids learn the basics through games, scrimmages, and fun drills. Make friends, gain confidence, love the sport. [Register]

**Tennis Revolution (NEW)** — Indoor 10U program with kid-friendly equipment, smaller courts, and games that actually teach serving and rallying. Confidence-builder. [Register]

**Charlotte Allstar Gymnastics & Cheerleading** — Gymnastics or cheerleading with games, crafts, and themed days (Tattoo Tuesday, Glow-in-the-Dark Thursday, Inflatable Fridays). Half-day and full-day options. $250/week, runs June 1 - August 21. [Register]

**Terrific Scientific** — 40+ hands-on science and engineering camps. Grades K-7. Multiple locations. [Register]

**Mad Science** — Hands-on STEM experiments and interactive science. PreK through 6th grade. Various locations, June 8 - August 7. [Register]

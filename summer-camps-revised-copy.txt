### Frequently Asked Questions

**What ages are these camps for?**
Most camps listed here serve ages 5-14, with分组ing by age within each program. Check individual camp pages for specific grade or age requirements.

**How much do summer camps cost?**
Costs vary by camp type and duration. Day camps typically range from $150-$400 per week. Specialty camps (like robotics or riding) may be higher. Click "Learn more" on each camp for current pricing.

**Are there before or after-care options?**
Many local camps offer extended hours — some as early as 7am and as late as 6pm. Ask when you register or check the camp's details page.

**What's the cancellation policy?**
Most camps offer partial refunds if you cancel 2 weeks before the session starts. Some offer credit toward future camps. Check with each program directly.

**Do siblings get a discount?**
Some camps offer sibling discounts — typically 10-15% off the second child's registration. It's worth asking when you sign up.

**Are meals or snacks included?**
Most day camps do NOT include meals — pack a lunch and snacks. Some half-day programs include snacks. Check individual camp pages for details.

# Squiggly Marketing Homepage Design Brief

## Visual Style
- #103a6d + 66c089 + white branding (trust, growth)
- Similar energy to Review Harvest — direct, no fluff
- "Set and forget" messaging throughout
- Testimonials prominent, above the fold if possible

## Hero Section
- **Headline:** "What's breaking your business?"
- **Subheadline:** Marketing that's supposed to bring customers, not just look pretty.
- **CTA Button:** "Tell me what's broken" → links to diagnostic form

---

## The Problem Section
Most local businesses have the same issues:
- Google listing is incomplete or ranked on page 3
- No system to ask for reviews
- Reviews exist but buried under bad ones
- Competitors show up first

---

### Tier 2: The Review Machine — $147/month

### Tier 3: Dominate Local — $297/month

## Benefits / Why Reviews Section
- Every 5-star review = ~1 new customer

## "Set It And Forget It" Section
- We build the system, YOUR team simply asks
- Email + SMS sequences handled automatically

- 3 clear tiers (Foundation, Review Machine, Dominate Local)

- "How long does setup take?"

## Design Notes
- Keep it clean, minimal, direct
- Use real photos of local Charlotte-area businesses (your own photos or stock)
- The diagnostic flow should ask 3-5 questions about their business
- Show problem → solution match based on their answers

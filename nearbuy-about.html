About The Local NearBuy

Your guide to the best of Greater Waxhaw — and a movement to feed children, one meal at a time.

Our Story

The Local NearBuy started with a simple observation: there's so much greatness in our community, but no one was telling those stories. Great coffee shops, hidden gem restaurants, reliable service businesses — they existed, but people didn't know about them.

At the same time, we wanted to build something that mattered. Not just another local directory, but a platform that actually made our community better.

💡 The Idea

What if every local business ad helped feed children? What if every deal redeemed made a difference? That's when Meals4Good was born.

Now, when a business advertises with The Local NearBuy, they're not just getting reach — they're helping provide meals to children who need them. Every ad space. Every redemption. Every interaction makes an impact.

This isn't just local marketing. It's a movement disguised as a directory. And you're invited to be part of it.

## Why It Works

1. **Google owns local search.** When someone searches "homes for sale near me" or "plumber in [city]," your GBP appears — or you get missed.

2. **Content alone isn't enough.** A website doesn't guarantee local visibility. You need the GBP foundation + citations + reviews.

3. **Consistency builds trust.** Same business name, address, and phone across 62+ directories = Google trusts you're real.

4. **Reviews drive conversions.** 4.8+ stars with keyword-rich text = more clicks, more calls.

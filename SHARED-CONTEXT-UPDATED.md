THE LOCAL NEARBUY
Primary active build. Hyperlocal advertising & directory platform for Greater Waxhaw, NC area.

FOUR ASSETS (all planned/built):
1. Social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram, Google Business Profile, LinkedIn) — daily posting
2. Directory website (WordPress) — business listings with claim system
3. Email newsletter — MailerLite, draft templates exist, not yet actively running
4. EDDM postcards — campaign tracking system built, first campaign not yet sold

BUSINESS MODEL:
- Advertising purchases tied to meal donations through Common Heart (local giving point in Waxhaw area) — NOT Meals4Good (scrapped)
- Businesses buying ad space/campaigns trigger donations, with The Local NearBuy named as contributor
- 80/20 split: 80% profit to Bryan, 20% to meals donated via Common Heart
- Revenue: $450/ad spot, 16 spots per campaign ($7,200 revenue, ~$4,800 profit)
- ~1,367 meals donated per campaign from profit + 655 meals built into ad revenue + 1 meal per redeemed offer

TECHNOLOGY (WordPress Plugin v3.7):
- Dynamic QR redirect system (/r/c001a05 → claim page)
- Campaign tracking (spots sold, meals guaranteed, redemptions tracked)
- Business dashboard, claim pages, voucher validation
- Free-only profile model (Pro/Pro+ tiers removed in May 2026)
- Google API images for ALL directory listings
- Download: https://github.com/squigglyweb/cleatus/raw/a1b9ae2/tln-plugin.zip

LEAD CAPTURE (Key Selling Point):
- Customer info (name/email/phone) collected at claim
- Opt-in checkbox: "Send me offers from [Business]"
- 100% opted-in leads — customers explicitly chose to hear from the business. Not cold calls or purchased lists.

CROSS-CHANNEL STRATEGY:
- Directory page shows "greyed out" offer teaser → drives curiosity
- Customer waits for/looks for the mailer
- Scans QR → unlocks full offer → "unwrapping" experience
- Loop: Directory teaser → Mailer scan → Claim → Redeem

VOUCHER STATUS INDICATORS:
- Green blink: >10 days remaining (active)
- Yellow blink: ≤10 days remaining
- Red blink: ≤7 days (grace period, expiring soon)
- Grey, no blink: Expired
- QR code shows under "Your Code" for staff to scan

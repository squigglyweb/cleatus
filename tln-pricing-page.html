What Each Feature Means

Directory Listing

Your business appears in our searchable directory when people look for services in Waxhaw and surrounding areas.

Custom Offers

Create special deals for your neighbors — "15% off", "Free appetizer", etc. Pro members get unlimited offers. Free members get none.

Photo Gallery

Upload your own photos to showcase your business, team, and services. Makes your listing stand out.

Video Embed

Add a YouTube or Vimeo video — a virtual tour, welcome message, or service explanation.

Priority Placement

Pro+ and Sponsor businesses appear at the top of search results. Free and Pro show lower down. Being first means more clicks.

Appointment Widget

Paste your Calendly, Square, Acuity, or Vagaro booking link directly on your profile. Customers can book instantly.

Newsletter Feature

Get mentioned in our weekly email to thousands of local neighbors. Pro+ members get a feature; Sponsors get an ad banner.

Postcard Campaign

We include your offer on our monthly postcard mailer. Pro+ gets a listing; Sponsors get a full ad spot.

Lead Capture Form

When someone fills out the "Send a Message" form on your profile, it goes straight to you via email.

Meals Counter

Show how many meals your business has provided to local families through our hunger relief program.

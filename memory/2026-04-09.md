# Memory: April 9, 2026

## nearbuy-postcard (The Local NearBuy)

### Project Overview
- Direct mail postcard advertising product for local businesses
- Front + Back = ad space for local businesses
- Includes meal donation component tied to redemptions
- Building toward Meals4Good non-profit

### Ad Space Inventory
**Front:**
- 1 Large: 7.8" × 3.8" → $800
- 2 Medium: 3.8" × 5" → $625 each
- 1 Small: 3.8" × 3.75" → $325

**Back:**
- 3 Small: 3.8" × 2.5" → $250 each
- 3 Medium: 3.8" × 5" → $625 each

**Total potential revenue: $5,000**

### Cost Structure
- Print cost: $2,344
- Max meals (capped at 2,000): 2,000 × $0.30 = $600
- M4G building fund: 2,000 × $0.20 = $400

### Financial Targets
- Target profit: $1,500 per campaign
- Revenue needed to hit target: $4,844
- Profit at full sellout: ~$1,656

### Meal Donation Structure
- Per redemption: 5 meals donated
- Cost per meal: $0.30 (food bank)
- M4G contribution per meal: $0.20 (building fund)
- Total per redemption: $0.50
- Cap: 2,000 meals per campaign ($1,000 max to cause)

### Pitch to Businesses
> "Your ad purchase does three things:
> 1. Gets your business on the postcard (print + distribution)
> 2. Feeds kids — we donate 5 meals when someone redeems your offer
> 3. Builds Meals4Good — 20% of meal funding goes to launching our non-profit"

### Front Messaging (Finalized)

# Meeting Questions — John Somers Real Estate

## Discovery
1. What's your website URL?
2. Have you done any SEO or ads before? What worked/didn't?
3. Where do most of your leads come from now? (Zillow, referrals, Google Business?)
4. How many deals did you close last year?
5. What's your goal — more leads? Better leads? Just visibility?

## Commitment
6. How much time can you commit to this? (Content, reviews, GBP posts)

## Budget & Scope
7. What's your budget for this?
8. What's your timeline expectation?
9. Are you looking for me to handle everything, or do some of the work yourself?

## Technical
10. Do you have login access to your website?
11. Do you have access to your Google Business Profile?
12. Any competitors you'd like to outrank?

## The Close
13. "Does this feel like a good fit? Let's get started."

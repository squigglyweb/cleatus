# The Local NearBuy - Postcard Pitch Script

## The Opener
"Hey [Name], I'm working on something new for local businesses in the Waxhaw area. Got 2 minutes?"

## What It Is
"The Local NearBuy is a hyperlocal advertising platform. We mail postcards to 5,000+ households in your neighborhood — people who can actually walk in and buy from you. No big newspaper waste, just focused local reach."

"Here's the thing — the postcard doesn't print the special offer. It just teases it. Customer scans a QR code, then SEES the offer for the first time. That scarcity drives action. They feel like they 'unwrapped' something exclusive."

"Interested in testing it with one route first? $250 to reach 5,000 nearby households with a tracked offer and qualified leads. What do you think?"

## Handle Objections

**"Too expensive"**
"What would it cost you to get 5,000 new eyes on your business any other way? This is targeted — these are your neighbors."

**"I already advertise"**
"Cool. How's that working? This is different — it's measurable. You see exactly who claimed the offer, and they're 100% interested because they chose to give their contact info."

**"I need to think about it"**
"Of course. Can I stop by Thursday afternoon? You can see the layout and decide then. No pressure."

# Podcast Prompt for NotebookLM

**Prompt:**

"Create a podcast episode (NO LONGER THAN 3 MINUTES) about The Local NearBuy, a hyperlocal media company serving Greater Waxhaw, North Carolina.

**What to cover:**
- What is The Local NearBuy? Your local guide to discovering the best of Waxhaw and surrounding areas
- For new residents: Moving to a new area means finding your new favorite coffee shop, pizza place, dentist, mechanic — TLN helps you find the locals' favorites, not just tourist traps
- Services offered: Social media features, online directory, EDDM postcards reaching thousands, newsletter, NearBuy Radio (streaming local jingles)
- The Meals4Good model: When customers scan QR codes on business ads, The Local NearBuy donates meals to local children — up to 2,000 meals per campaign
- Content pillars: Coffee Corner, Pizza Files, Date Night Worthy, The Sweet Spot, Home Heroes, Smile Squad, Hidden Gems, Kid-Friendly Favorites
- The mission: Shop local, feed families, support community
- The founder: A local 10+ year resident, longtime graphic designer who loves his community and wanted to create something meaningful for his neighbors
- Current status: Just launched, building content, growing the local business network

**Tone:** Friendly, welcoming, like a helpful neighbor

**Audience:** Both longtime residents and newcomers to the area

**Call to action:** Know a great local business? Recommend it in the form below or at thelocalnearbuy.com/contact — we love featuring hidden gems"

"And we're going to take a quick break. Don't go anywhere — we'll be right back!"

Every time you check out one of our recommendations, we donate a meal to a local child in need. Shop local, feed families. Learn more at thelocalnearbuy.com."

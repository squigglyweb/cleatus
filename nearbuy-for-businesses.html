Frequently Asked Questions

How does EDDM work?

Every Door Direct Mail lets us mail to every household in your target area — no mailing list required. We handle everything.

When do I need to submit my offer?

Offers are due 2 weeks before the mailing date. We'll send you a proof before we print.

How do I track redemptions?

Each business gets a unique QR code. Customers scan it to redeem, and we track every scan.

Can I run multiple campaigns?

Yes! Many businesses run quarterly campaigns. Each one is billed separately.

What if I need help with my offer?

Our team will help craft an offer that converts. That's included in the price.

## Revenue Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Conservative
- 10 Pro ($99) → $990/mo
- 5 Pro+ ($199) → $995/mo
- 2 Sponsors ($349) → $698/mo
- **Total:** $2,683/mo

### Scenario 2: Break Even Covered
- 15 Pro ($99) → $1,485/mo
- 10 Pro+ ($199) → $1,990/mo
- 2 Sponsors ($349) → $698/mo
- **Total:** $4,173/mo

### Add-On Revenue (A La Carte)
- Postcard ads: $75-600/campaign × 2 = $150-1,200/mo
- Newsletter banners: $25-75/email × 52 = $1,300-3,900/mo

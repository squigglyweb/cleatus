## Post 5 - Friday (Big Ron's)

**Platform:** Facebook + Instagram  
**Type:** Story + single image

---

**This is the last independently owned fish fry spot on this entire stretch of road.**

Big Ron's has been here since 2001. That's 25 years. Ron's father was a catfish farmer — so Ron knows his fish. The hush puppies are from his grandmother's recipe, and she'll tell you that herself if you ask.

This is a Black-owned business that's been feeding this community for a quarter century. When local fundraisers happen, Ron donates. When kids need lunch, Ron provides.

That's not just a restaurant. That's a neighbor.

📍 8500 u-Center Drive

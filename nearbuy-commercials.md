# The Local NearBuy - Podcast Commercials & Ad Spots

## 30-Second Ad Spots

---

**SPOT 1: Introduction**
> "Are you new to Waxhaw? Or maybe you've lived here for years but feel like you're missing out on some great local spots? The Local NearBuy is your guide to the best of Greater Waxhaw — from coffee corners to pizza joints, from date night to family fun. We feature the businesses you love, and every time you check out one of our recommendations, we donate a meal to a local child in need. Shop local, feed families. Find us at thelocalnearbuy.com."

---

**SPOT 2: Meals4Good**
> "Here's what makes The Local NearBuy different. Every time you scan a QR code from one of our featured businesses, we donate a meal to a child in our community. Yep — simply by being a customer at your favorite local spot, you're helping feed families. That's the NearBuy difference. Shop local, feed families. Learn more at thelocalnearbuy.com."

---

**SPOT 3: For Business Owners**
> "Hey business owners — want more locals walking through your door? The Local NearBuy can help. We feature local businesses on social media, in our directory, and on our quarterly postcards mailed to thousands of households. And here's the best part: every ad helps feed kids in need. It's advertising that gives back. Learn more at thelocalnearbuy.com."

---

**SPOT 4: New Resident Welcome**
> "New to the area? Welcome to Greater Waxhaw! There's so much to discover here — amazing locally-owned restaurants, hidden gems you've probably walked past a hundred times, and businesses that truly care about this community. The Local NearBuy is your insider guide to it all. Find your new favorite spots at thelocalnearbuy.com."

**Coming Back:**
> "And we're back! Thanks for sticking around. Now, let's talk about..."

> "Quick break — we'll be right back."

**Sponsor Read Placeholder:**

## End of Episode Tag

> "Thanks for listening to The Local NearBuy. If you know a great local business we should feature, drop us a line at thelocalnearbuy.com. That's thelocalnearbuy.com. Shop local, feed families, and we'll see you next time!"

## Voiceover Notes

- **Tone:** Friendly, warm, like a neighbor who knows the best spots
- **Pacing:** Clear and deliberate, ~2.5 words per second for 30-second spots
- **Music bed:** Can use "TLN Anthem" or similar for intro/outro

---

## Usage Tips

1. **Spot 1** — Best for intro/episodes explaining what TLN is
2. **Spot 2** — Use when discussing Meals4Good model
3. **Spot 3** — For episodes targeting business owners
4. **Spot 4** — Perfect for episodes about new residents

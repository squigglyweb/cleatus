# The Local NearBuy — Weekly Operating System

## THE MANDATE
**Mission:** Feed our community. Every ad donates meals. People advertise BECAUSE of the cause, not just "because."

---

## Daily System (What You Do Each Day)

| Day | Focus | Action |
|-----|-------|--------|
| **Mon** | **Business outreach** | Contact 1-2 local businesses about postcard ads |
| **Tue** | **Content creation** | Write/update 1 article or guide |
| **Wed** | **Social batch** | Schedule week's social posts |
| **Thu** | **Newsletter** | Assemble & send Thursday newsletter |
| **Fri** | **Community engagement** | Reply to comments, check FB groups, engage |
| **Sat** | **Observation** | Walk around town, notice things, take photos |
| **Sun** | **Planning** | Review week, plan next week's theme |

---

## Weekly Content Pillars (Rotate Through)

1. **History Week** — Andrew Jackson, Museum of the Waxhaws, JAARS, Gold Hill mining, Marvin origins
2. **Camps & Kids** — Summer camps, VBS, school schedules, kid activities
3. **Hidden Gems** — "Things right under your feet" — places locals don't know about
4. **Food & Drink** — Restaurant features, new openings, food truck roundups
5. **Weekend Guide** — What's happening Fri-Sun
6. **Local Business Spotlight** — Feature one business deeply
7. **New Resident Welcome** — Guide for people new to the area

**Pattern:** Rotate through these so there's always something fresh.

---

## Newsletter Flow (What Comes First)

**Option A: Content first, then curate**
- You write the main piece (history, guide, etc.)
- Then you curate other local happening around it
- This is BETTER — gives you a anchor piece each week

**Option B: Curate first**
- Round up links and events
- Weaker — just a list, no reason to read

**GO WITH: Option A**
- Each newsletter has ONE main feature article
- Everything else supports it

## Monetization Layers

1. **Postcard ads** — Primary revenue ($2,050/campaign profit)
2. **Directory listings** — Monthly/annual fees
3. **Newsletter sponsorships** — Featured business $X/week
4. **Your merch** — Town-themed products you design
5. **Service referrals** — Partner with providers, take margin
6. **Cause pride** — Businesses WANT to participate because meals donated

## The Key Differentiator

Every time you talk to a business:

That's the pitch. That's the pride.
